02The brief

What the client wanted.

"We're building the next generation of AI models — and they need eyes. Can you give us a device that captures the world the way a person sees it, all day, without anyone noticing they're wearing it?"

The ask was three ideas stacked on top of each other. Capture first-person video + audio + motion at research fidelity. Do it for a full working day on one charge. And make it light and neutral enough that a contributor would forget it was there within the first hour.

The second constraint killed every off-the-shelf option. The third killed most of the first round of concepts.
